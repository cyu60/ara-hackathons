Overview

Why Host a Build Day

With support from Ara, we're hosting a Build Day on campus — a one-day experience where students from different backgrounds come together to experiment, build, and imagine new possibilities with AI.

This is an opportunity for your community to not just learn about AI, but to actively shape how it can be used in creative, meaningful ways.

Whether you're a computer science student, a researcher, or simply curious — we'd love to create a space for you to explore and build together. No prior experience needed — just curiosity.

  • A fully organized Build Day hackathon on your campus — we handle everything
  • Free catering, prizes, mentorship, and swag for all participants
  • Hands-on experience building with Ara's AI personal computer
  • Open to all students — CS, humanities, business, design, and beyond
  • Join a cross-university builder community after the event
About Ara

Your Personal AI Agent

Ara (YC X26) is a personal AI agent that works 24/7 across WhatsApp, iMessage, Signal, and Telegram. One download gives users access to Claude, GPT, and Gemini through a privacy-first, cross-messenger interface.

Ara is actively targeting the university market — already adopted at Stanford, UC Berkeley, Virginia Tech, and Carnegie Mellon. This campus tour accelerates that momentum across 6 additional top-tier institutions.

Part A.3 — Event Format

6-Hour Builder Sprint

Each campus stop follows a repeatable six-hour format — short enough for strong student turnout, long enough for real product usage and demos, and easy to replicate across campuses.

Activity Duration
Kickoff & Live Ara Demo 30 min
Idea Formation & Team Matching 45 min
Guided Build Session 3 hours
Mentor Feedback 45 min
Demos & Judging 60 min
Closing & Community Onboarding 30 min
  • Short enough for strong student turnout
  • Long enough for real product usage and demos
  • Easy to repeat across campuses
  • Lighter operationally than a 24–48 hour hackathon
  • Well suited to a multi-campus roadshow
